## Citation Guidelines

### In-Text Citations

**Single author:**
- (Brooks, 1991)
- Brooks (1991) argues that...

**Two authors:**
- (Pfeifer & Bongard, 2006)
- Pfeifer and Bongard (2006) demonstrate...

**Three or more authors:**
- (Brohan et al., 2023)
- Brohan et al. (2023) present...

**Direct quotes:**
- "Intelligence without representation" (Brooks, 1991, p. 140)

### Reference List Format

**Journal article:**
Author, A. A., & Author, B. B. (Year). Title of article. *Title of Journal*, volume(issue), pages. https://doi.org/xxx

**Book:**
Author, A. A. (Year). *Title of book*. Publisher.

**Conference paper:**
Author, A. A., & Author, B. B. (Year). Title of paper. In *Conference name* (pp. pages). Publisher.

**Website/Documentation:**
Organization. (Year). *Title*. Retrieved from URL
