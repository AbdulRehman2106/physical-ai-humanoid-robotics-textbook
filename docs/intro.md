# Welcome to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the comprehensive guide to Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics. This textbook covers everything from fundamental concepts to advanced implementation techniques.

## What You'll Learn

- **Physical AI Fundamentals**: Embodied intelligence, sensor-motor loops, and the reality gap
- **ROS 2 Development**: Nodes, topics, services, actions, and lifecycle management
- **Simulation Platforms**: Gazebo, NVIDIA Isaac Sim, and platform comparisons
- **Vision-Language-Action Models**: Multimodal AI for robotics
- **Sim-to-Real Transfer**: Domain randomization and Digital Twins

## Course Structure

This textbook is organized into 12 chapters designed for a 12-week learning journey:

- **Weeks 1-2**: Physical AI Fundamentals
- **Weeks 3-4**: ROS 2 Development
- **Weeks 5-7**: Simulation Workflows
- **Week 8**: Vision-Language-Action Models
- **Weeks 9-10**: Sim-to-Real Transfer
- **Week 11**: Error Handling & Troubleshooting
- **Week 12**: Capstone Project

## Prerequisites

- Basic programming knowledge (Python or C++)
- Computer capable of running ROS 2 and simulations
- 3-5 hours per week for 12 weeks

## Getting Started

Begin with [Chapter 1: Introduction to Physical AI](./chapters/01-physical-ai-intro/) to start your journey into embodied intelligence and robotics systems.
