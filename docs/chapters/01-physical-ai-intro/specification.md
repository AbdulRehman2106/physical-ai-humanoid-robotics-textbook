# Chapter 1 Specification: Introduction to Physical AI

**Chapter Number**: 1
**Title**: Introduction to Physical AI
**Estimated Reading Time**: 45-60 minutes
**Difficulty Level**: Beginner
**Prerequisites**: Basic understanding of AI concepts

## Learning Outcomes

By the end of this chapter, students will be able to:

1. **Define Physical AI** and explain how it differs from traditional AI systems
2. **Describe embodied intelligence** and its role in robotics
3. **Explain sensor-motor loops** and their importance in autonomous systems
4. **Identify the reality gap** between simulation and real-world robotics
5. **Recognize applications** of Physical AI in industry and research

   - Info: Physical AI definition

   - Q1: What distinguishes Physical AI from traditional AI?

3. **Checkpoint** (5 items):
